Features

Built different, on purpose

Instant response

Engineered for raw performance. Pages load faster, tabs stay light, and everything feels immediate.

Private by default

Trackers blocked. Fingerprinting prevented. Your browsing stays yours without any configuration.

Open foundation

Built on Chromium with full transparency. Every decision we make is visible, auditable, and intentional.

Clean interface

A minimal UI that stays out of your way. Focus on content, not chrome around the content.

Extensible

Full support for the extensions you already use. Compatible with the Chrome Web Store ecosystem.

Cross-platform

Available on every major platform. Your browser follows you across desktop and mobile seamlessly.

Philosophy

We believe the browser can be better

The browser is the most used software on every device, yet it has barely evolved in a decade. We're changing that.

Herbert strips away the unnecessary and rebuilds what matters. Faster rendering, stronger privacy, and a design that respects your attention.
